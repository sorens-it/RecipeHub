Mic's Yorkshire Puds

Ingredients

  • 200g plain flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 300ml milk
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Put the flour and some seasoning into a large bowl
  2. Stir in eggs, one at a time
  3. Whisk in milk until you have a smooth batter
  4. Chill in the fridge for at least 30 minutes(30 minutes)
  5. Heat oven to 220°C/425°F (gas mark 7)
  6. Pour the oil into the holes of an 8-hole muffin tin
  7. Heat tin in the oven for 5 minutes(5 minutes)
  8. Ladle the batter mix into the tin
  9. Bake for 30 minutes until well browned and risen(30 minutes)
