Curried Chicken Salad

Ingredients

  • 3 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, halved lengthwise
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp lemon zest
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 tsp curry powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 ripe mangoes, diced
  • 1/4 cup dried cranberries
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 6 leaves Boston lettuce
  • 6 English muffins, toasted

Instructions

  1. Arrange chicken in a single layer in a large pot
  2. Add water to just cover
  3. Bring to a boil over medium-high
  4. Flip chicken, reduce heat to medium and simmer until cooked(6 minutes)
  5. Cool cooked chicken
  6. Stir mayo with lemon zest, juice, curry and salt in large bowl
  7. Shred chicken and mix with mayo mixture, mango, cranberries, onions and celery
  8. Divide mixture among muffins with lettuce leaves
  9. Sandwich with muffin tops and serve
