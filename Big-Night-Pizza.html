Big Night Pizza

Ingredients

  • 5 teaspoons yeast
  • 5 cups flour
  • 4 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 cups hot water
  • 1/4 cup pizza sauce
  • 3/4 cup mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Add hot water to yeast in a large bowl and let sit for 15 minutes(15 minutes)
  2. Mix in oil, sugar, salt, and flour and let sit for 1 hour(60 minutes)
  3. Knead the dough and spread onto a pan
  4. Spread pizza sauce and sprinkle cheese
  5. Add any optional toppings as you wish
  6. Bake at 400°F for 15 minutes(15 minutes)
  7. Enjoy!
