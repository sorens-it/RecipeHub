Blueberry Oatmeal Squares

Ingredients

  • 2-1/2 cups rolled oats (not instant)
  • 1-1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp grated orange rind
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup cold butter, cubed
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 3 cups fresh blueberries
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup orange juice
  • 4 tsp cornstarch

Instructions

  1. Filling: In saucepan, bring blueberries, sugar and orange juice to boil; reduce heat and simmer until tender(10 minutes)
  2. Whisk cornstarch with 2 tbsp water; whisk into blueberries and boil until thickened(1 minute)
  3. Place plastic wrap directly on surface; refrigerate until cooled(60 minutes)
  4. In large bowl, whisk together oats, flour, sugar, orange rind and salt
  5. With pastry blender, cut in butter until coarse crumbs form
  6. Press half into 8-inch square parchment-lined cake pan; spread with blueberry filling
  7. Bake at 350°F until light golden(45 minutes)
  8. Let cool on rack before cutting into squares
  9. (Make-ahead: Refrigerate for up to 2 days or freeze for 2 weeks)
